## 🚀 About Me

I'm a data engineer and entrepreneur based in **Nairobi, Kenya**, specializing in building robust data infrastructure and scalable systems. Currently serving as **Lead Data Engineer at [Ardhisasa](https://ardhisasa.go.ke)**—Kenya's National Land Information Management System—where I architect data solutions that serve millions of citizens.

With **5+ years of professional experience** spanning IoT platforms, cloud infrastructure, and data engineering, I focus on creating systems that are both powerful and maintainable. As co-founder of **[Codetricraft](https://codetricraft.co.ke)**, I lead a team delivering affordable, custom software solutions to SMEs across Kenya.

**Current Focus:** Implementing **LLM workflows for business applications**, bridging the gap between traditional data engineering and modern AI capabilities.
